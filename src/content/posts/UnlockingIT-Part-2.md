As I looked for ways to speed up development, I tested the usual suspects: [WordPress](https://wordpress.com/) and [Wix](https://www.wix.com/). They were fine, but they felt restrictive. Then I found [Webflow](https://webflow.com/).

It became the bridge between design and development I had been looking for.

Soon, clients started asking for "Buy Now" buttons. I needed to move from static brochures to dynamic e-commerce, but I didn't want to abandon my static site workflow.

I evaluated [Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/), [CS-Cart](https://www.cs-cart.com/), and [SnipCart](https://snipcart.com/), but landed on [Ecwid](https://www.ecwid.com/).
